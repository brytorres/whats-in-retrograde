What's In
Retrograde?

What does is mean when a planet goes retrograde? Retrograde motion is an illusion in which a planet seems to stop and begin to move backwards. It does not mean the planet ACTUALLY begins to start backwards in its orbit. It just appears to do so because of the relative positions of the planet and Earth and how they are moving around the Sun.

Apart from the astronomical meaning of retrograde movement, there is also an astrological significance. Sometimes things tend to go haywire in our lives without any apparent reason. Well, look up at the sky and you may just find the reason. When a planet is retrograde, its energies seem to affect us in a different way and are turned inward and becomes more subjective.
